About the Mandir

Bhagwati Sthan Mandir is a sacred temple dedicated to Maa Bhagwati. It has been a center for spiritual growth, cultural celebrations, and community welfare for decades.

Devotees gather here for festivals like Navratri, Kartik Purnima, and Makar Sankranti. The temple not only provides a place for worship but also supports social welfare initiatives, bringing the community together in service and devotion.
